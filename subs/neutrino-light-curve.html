Neutrino Light Curves of Core-Collapse Supernovae

This is a poster I presented at the 2021 APS April Meeting. The poster is about the neutrino light curves of core-collapse supernovae. The poster is based on the paper "Neutrino Light Curves of Core-Collapse Supernovae" by K. Nakazato, K. Sumiyoshi, H. Suzuki, T. Totani, H. Umeda, and S. Yamada. The paper is published in the Astrophysical Journal, 789: 127 (2014). The poster is created using LaTeX and TikZ.
